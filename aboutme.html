My name is Mahtab, and I am one of the many advocates for the wild horses and burros
that reside within the United States.

I created this blog to shine lighy on the Bureau of Land Management's (BLM) current
poor system of managment of wild mustangs and burros, to discuss their welfare
to promote the preservation and protection of America's wild equines and to simply raise
awareness.
